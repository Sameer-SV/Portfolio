Projects

Trivia Chase

This was a group project Made in Java (Co-Author: Eric Dyk), its a fun quiz game inspired by Kahoot, where you choose a category like Food, History, Science, or Animals and answer 5 questions per round. Questions include multiple types such as word guessing, drag-and-drop, slider/number guessing, and true or false. Points are awarded based on performance, and a leaderboard keeps track of high scores, saved directly within the game.
